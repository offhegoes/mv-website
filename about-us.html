ABOUT US

Micro Venture (M) Sdn Bhd (MV) is an IT firm based in Putrajaya, Malaysia. Since its presence in 2009, MV has partnered with various global tech companies to provide the best modern world technology solution and bring in new digital experiences to our esteemed clients.

At MV, we specialise in delivering innovative, scalable, and tailored digital solutions for businesses and organisations across various sectors. Our core offerings span across custom software development, system integration, and digital transformation services designed to empower our clients through technology.

To deliver comprehensive and robust digital solutions, MV collaborates with a network of trusted partners across key technology domains. These partnerships enable us to provide end-to-end services that meet the evolving demands of our clients.

We work with established hardware vendors to supply reliable infrastructure components that ensure performance and scalability.

Our network system partners support the design and deployment of secure, high-availability connectivity for both on-premises and cloud environments.

In the area of cybersecurity, we align with leading security solution providers to safeguard digital assets and ensure compliance with industry standards. For software, we integrate with proven off-the-shelf systems to accelerate deployment, while also collaborating with specialized teams on custom system development to meet unique business needs.
